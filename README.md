## Map Information

This interactive map was created in **R** using the leaflet package and
exported as a standalone HTML document. It visualizes the movements of
satellite-tagged fin whales (*Balaenoptera physalus*) tracked from the
North-Western Mediterranean Sea to adjacent Atlantic waters.

The map includes selectable and deselectable layers that allow users to
explore whale movements, inferred behavioral states, and their overlap
with conservation and management areas.

## Conservation and Management Layers

-   **North-Western Mediterranean Particularly Sensitive Sea Area
    (PSSA)**

-   **Important Marine Mammal Areas (IMMAs)**

-   **Specially Protected Areas of Mediterranean Importance (SPAMIs)**

-   **Land-Based Observatory in Dénia, Eastern Spain**

## Fin Whale Movement Layers

-   **HMM Positions by PTT ID**\
    Regularized fin whale locations derived from a Hidden Markov Model
    (HMM), colored according to the satellite tag (PTT) identifier.

-   **Whale Tracks (HMM)**\
    Movement tracks reconstructed from the HMM

-   **Whale Behavioral States (HMM)**\
    Locations colored according to behavioral states inferred by the
    HMM.

-   **hDCRWS Positions by PTT ID**\
    Regularized fin whale locations estimated using a hierarchical
    first-Difference Correlated Random Walk with Switching (hDCRWS)
    model, colored according to the PTT identifier.

-   **Whale Tracks (hDCRWS)**\
    Movement tracks reconstructed from the hDCRWS model.

-   **Whale Behavioral States (hDCRWS)**\
    Locations colored according to behavioral states inferred by the
    hDCRWS model.
